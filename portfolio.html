Strategy

How I approach every project

01

Research the real problem

I start by clarifying user needs, product goals, and the points where the current journey breaks down.

02

Structure the flow

I map the experience into screens, states, and decisions so the interface has a clear path before visual polish begins.

03

Prototype the feeling

I use motion, microcopy, and interaction details to test whether the product feels simple and intentional.

04

Refine the system

I turn the strongest direction into reusable patterns that stay consistent as the product grows.
